Rules and
Regulations

Essay writing entails much more than an academic endeavour. It is a thought provoking-venture that engages both the reader and the writer. YESM provides a diverse platform that encourages these promising writers from all fields of study to showcase their literary prowess.

Topics:

  1. Is India on a sustainable path to societal and economic progress?
  2. Increase in female labour force participation post-liberalisation: A baby step or a giant stride in women‚Äôs empowerment.
  3. Cyber-feminism in India: Role of social media movements in women‚Äôs empowerment and gender equality.

Rules and Format:

  1. The event is open to enrolled high school (+2) and undergraduate students of any discipline.
  2. The event will be held online.
  3. The essay must be an individual work of the author. No collaborations are allowed.
  4. Participants must write an essay on any one of the given topics.
  5. One participant can submit only one essay. Multiple submissions from the same participant are not allowed.
  6. Any plagiarism shall lead to immediate disqualification.
  7. The last date of submission for the essay is 16th January, 2023.
  8. The essay must not exceed a word count of 1600 words (including in-text citations but excluding cover page, footnotes, diagram or image captions, tables if any, and end-text citations).
  9. Participants will receive a participant number via email upon registering. Therefore, at the time of submission, they should clearly mention their participation number and topic title on the cover page/first page of the essay
  10. Participants should submit the essay via email to the designated email address - pawrito@yesm.in with the subject as Pawrito_NameOfTheParticipant.
  11. Participants should use the APA referencing style to cite other papers and articles.
  12. Submission can be in either .pdf or word format.

Not abiding by the aforementioned rules or format may lead to disqualification.
